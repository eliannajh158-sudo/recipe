汤圆 Cookie

Directory:

  • Ingredients
  • Procedures

Ingredients (for 3 cookies):

  • 3 tang yuan (汤圆)
  • 28g butter
  • 45g flour (any type)
  • 20g sugar
  • 0.5g salt
  • 0.3g baking powder (泡打粉)
  • 0.3g baking soda (小苏打)
  • 1 room temperature egg (常温鸡蛋)
  • 4g milk
  • 1g flavoured powder (optional)
  • toppings you like e.g. chocolate chips, nuts (optional)

Procedures:

  1. Put the tang yuan in the freezer (冻汤圆)
  2. Melt the butter until can be pressed down easily and put in a bowl (软化黄油)
  3. Pour the flour, sugar, salt, baking powder and baking soda into the bowl with the butter
  4. Crack the room temperature egg into the bowl and pour in the milk
  5. Mix everything together until a dough is formed
  6. Split the dough into smaller pieces about 35g each (每坨35g左右)
  7. Add the flavoured powder and mix well (optional)
  8. Flatten the smaller dough and wrap around the frozen tang yuan (面团压扁，把冻汤圆包进去)
  9. Add on the toppings (optional)
  10. Place the cookies on a plate with some spacing in between each other and put in the microwave (把饼干比较有距离的摆在盘子上，放进微波炉)
  11. Microwave on HIGH for 2 minutes (微波炉高火2分钟)
